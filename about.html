Carol Coates

Logsden Country Store is located in the foothills of the Oregon Coast Mountain Range, serving the small community of Logsden, Or. It has been in the Coates Family since the 1950's, currently being run by Carol Coates.

Carol is not only a business owner she is also a post master and avid hiker. She knows the area better than anyone else.

Moonshine Park

Moonshine Park is located just 4 miles down Moonshine Park Rd just across the Bridge from the Logsden Store. The Park has 38 sites with picnic tables and fire pits, multiple campsites, 2 group campsites, 3 restrooms, 2 pull through RV sites, boat launch, horseshoe pits, access to the river and much more.

The Park is open year road, providing great outdoor activities! In the summertime, the Logsden Store carries inner tubes (rent and buy) which are fun to use for floating down the Siletz and also just to lounge around the swimming holes at the park.

In the winter, the park has some of the nicest fishing holes in the area.

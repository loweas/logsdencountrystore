    Logsden Country Store

    The Logsden Country Store is a community convenience store and post office nestled in the Siletz River valley. The Coates family has owned and operated the business since 1952!

    In addition to groceries, the Logsden Store also provides conveniences such as gas, propane, beer, wine, including a wonderful selection of microbrews, a hot deli for folks on the run or fresh made deli sandwiches with homemade potato or pasta salads for those who can wait a few minutes. We are a licensing service for the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife and also carry a nice supply of bait and tackle. For local farmers there is an array of feed for chickens, rabbits, cattle, and horses.

    The area is a sportsman’s playground where hunting and fishing abounds. Lincoln County’s Moonshine Campground is just up the road and offers camping sites for tents and motor homes.

    Feed Selection
